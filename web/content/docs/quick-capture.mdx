# Quick Capture

TodoFocus's most immediate feature: a global hotkey that works from anywhere on your Mac, even when the app is in the background.

## Using ⌘⇧T

Press **`⌘⇧T`** from any application. A floating capture panel slides down from the top of your screen.

1. Type your thought
2. Press **Enter** to save

The panel closes automatically. You're back to what you were doing.

> [!note]
> The global hotkey requires **Accessibility permission**. On first use, macOS will prompt you to grant it in **System Settings → Privacy & Security → Accessibility**. This is a macOS requirement for monitoring keystrokes system-wide.

## Where Captures Go

- **If Deep Focus is active** — the capture is appended as a timestamped note to your active focus task. This lets you capture interruptions without breaking your flow.
- **If no Deep Focus is running** — a new task is created in your Inbox.

## Voice Capture

Inside the Quick Capture panel, click the **microphone icon** to switch to voice mode.

- Speak your thought and TodoFocus transcribes it in real time
- Tap the mic again or pause for 1.6 seconds to auto-submit
- Works in English (`en-US`)

> [!warning]
> Voice capture requires **Microphone** and **Speech Recognition** permissions in **System Settings → Privacy & Security**.

## Natural Language Quick Add

You can type short commands directly into Quick Capture or the task list Quick Add field:

| Input | Result |
|-------|--------|
| `Write report !` | Task "Write report" marked Important |
| `Call mom tomorrow` | Task "Call mom" due tomorrow |
| `Review doc @myday` | Task "Review doc" in My Day |
| `Presentation ! next week` | Important task due next week |

Tokens are highlighted as you type so you can verify what TodoFocus understood.

## Keyboard Reference

| Key | Action |
|-----|--------|
| `⌘⇧T` | Open Quick Capture (global) |
| `Enter` | Submit capture |
| `Esc` | Dismiss panel without saving |

## Troubleshooting

**⌘⇧T doesn't work**
: Check that TodoFocus has Accessibility permission in **System Settings → Privacy & Security → Accessibility**. If the app was recently re-signed or reinstalled, you may need to re-grant permission.

**Voice capture isn't transcribing**
: Ensure **Speech Recognition** is enabled in **System Settings → Privacy & Security → Speech**.

**Capture goes to the wrong task**
: Make sure no Deep Focus session is running, or check which task is selected as the focus target in the task detail panel.
